FiFish

FiFish is probably my dearest personal project, because it is also my first (more serious) one. It is purely a frontend website, made using the public FishWatch.gov API. The concept is very simple: just search for a fish (or seafood) and you will see lots of interesting facts about the species, such as appearance, biology and a surprising collection of pictures.
